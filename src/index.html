Battleship

Objective

Be the first to sink all 5 of your opponent's ships.

Preparation

  1. Click the Start Game button to play
  2. To place your ships, click the Place Ships button Continue to press this button until you are ready.
  3. Click the Ready button to start the game

Playing the Game

You will start the game by clicking on a coordinate on your target board. Then you will either 'Hit' or 'Miss' your opponent's ships. Then your opponent will attack.
A ship will sink when you have hit all coordinates that it is placed on. It will highlight indicating it has sunk.

Winning the Game

The first player to sink their opponent's fleet of 5 ships wins!

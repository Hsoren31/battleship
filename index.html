Battleship

Objective of the Game

Be the first to sink all 5 of your opponent's ships.

Preparation

  1. To place your ships, click the 'Place Ships' button until you are satisfied with their placement.
  2. Click the 'Ready' button to start the game

Playing the Game

  • To give an attack, click on a coordinate on your 'Target Board'
  • You will recieve a 'Hit' or 'Miss' from your opponent. Signifying whether you hit or missed their ship.
  • Your opponent will then attack you.

Sinking a Ship

To sink a ship you must hit all coordinates it is located on.

Winning the Game

The first player to sink their opponent's fleet of 5 ships wins!
